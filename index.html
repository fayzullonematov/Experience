Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our trading services

How do I get started with trading?

Getting started is easy. Simply sign up for an account, choose your preferred trading plan, and follow our step-by-step tutorials. We recommend starting with our educational resources to build a solid foundation before making your first trade.

What trading platforms do you support?

We support all major trading platforms including MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, TradingView, and our proprietary Gtrade platform. Our signals and strategies can be implemented on any of these platforms.

How accurate are your trading signals?

Our trading signals have a historical accuracy of 82-87%. However, it's important to understand that all trading involves risk, and past performance is not indicative of future results. We provide comprehensive risk management guidelines with each signal.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or hidden fees. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your current plan until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer one-on-one coaching?

Yes, we offer personalized one-on-one coaching sessions for traders who want dedicated guidance. These sessions are available to Elite plan subscribers or can be purchased separately. Contact our support team for more information.

What is the minimum capital required to start trading?

While you can start with as little as $100, we recommend a minimum capital of $1,000 to properly implement our strategies with adequate risk management. Remember that trading with very small capital can limit your ability to diversify and manage risk effectively.
